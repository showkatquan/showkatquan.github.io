About

A little about me

I graduated with an Integrated B.Sc.–M.Sc. in Physics from the Central University of Kashmir. During my studies, I gradually found myself drawn towards quantum physics and the questions it raises about the nature of the physical world.

Research internships and independent learning introduced me to quantum information, open quantum systems, and quantum computing. Every experience has taught me something different and continues to shape the way I approach research.

I know I am still at the beginning of my research journey, and there is a great deal for me to learn. That is what motivates me the most.
